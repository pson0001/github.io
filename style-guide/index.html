Intro

During the development of various projects, a major problem I am facing is to keep the style and use of classes consistent.

I am developing this style guide to help manage and maintain the visual style of this website and to provide a template for any future projects I will be working on.

This site utilises HAML and SASS with the SCSS syntax, and adopts the principle from functional CSS.
