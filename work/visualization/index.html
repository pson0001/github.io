Intro

My Project is exploring women's clothing online shopping behaviour from a Women's Clothing E-Commerce which revolving around the reviews written by customers. After exploration of dataset, the most interesting is the behavior of what women purchase and what they are talking about are quick different. Also, the main purchasing age group are 30-40 and 40-50 instead of younger generation.

As known as the digital innovation, rising globalization, and changes in consumer spending habits have totally changed the fashion industry into a new era. Analysis and explore in real word dataset helps to find more useful inside.
